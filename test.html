GasCanBase 2.0 Team


Our aim is to be the Internet's most trusted resource for Gastric Cancer related health information. We will achieve this aim by presenting independent, objective, comprehensive and up-to-date information in a clear and concise format for researchers.

Our team focused on researches on Metagenomes, Cancer Biology, Neurobiology, Microbial Informatics etc. using state of the art technologies, to reach professional & international standard on Bioinformtics and Computational Biology Arena.
